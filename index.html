About Me

Welcome to you visitor, my name is Kevin Bouchard, I am native to the province of Quebec in Canada and I am currently an assistant Professor at the Université du Québec à Chicoutimi. I am an active member of the Laboratoire d'Intelligence Ambiante pour la Reconnaissance d'Activités (LIARA). Our team is focused on ambient intelligence through the development of multimodal smart homes. These smart homes aim to provide day to day assistance to semi-autonomous residents in order to enhance their quality of life and promote autonomy. The LIARA team also focus on developing smaller assistive technologies targeted at smaller restrained sets of problems (e.g.: independent smart stove to assist while cooking).

Research Interests

My main interests of research include:

  • Data mining
  • Qualitative Spatial Reasoning
  • Localization and tracking
  • Ambient intelligence
